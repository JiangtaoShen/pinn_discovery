Physics-Informed Neural Networks

PINN Discovery

PINN Discovery automates the end-to-end design of physics-informed neural networks with two key innovations. PUCT tree search treats every candidate as a complete, runnable method — loss formulation, network architecture, and training strategy in one program — trains it under a strict wall-clock budget, scores it against a held-out reference, and keeps improving along the paths that pay off. A knowledge base of 130 PINN papers grounds the search: it seeds the tree with faithful re-implementations and steers every step as inspiration.
